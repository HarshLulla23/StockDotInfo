1.)What is a Stock ?

A stock or share (also known as a company's "equity") is afinancial instrument that represents ownership(simply a person who hold the shares,owns a slice of the company equal to the number of shares proportionately) in a company or corporation and represents a proportionate claim on its assets(what it owns) and earnings (what it generates in profits).

2.)What is a Stock Market ?

A stock market is the aggregation of buyers and sellers of stocks listed on a public stock exchange.

3.)What is an IPO ?

An IPO(Intinial Public Offering) is the process by which a private company can go public by sale of its stocks to general public.

4.)How many sectors are there to invest in the Stock Market ?

You can invest in 11 different sectors in the stock market. This type of industry categorization helps the portfolio manager create a diverse portfolio and allocate funds more efficiently.

5.)What is a Demat account ?

A demat account is an account to hold financial securities(stocks, commodities,etc) in electronic form.In India, depositories such as NSDL and CDSL provide Free Demat account services.

6.)Why to invest in Stock Market ?

Among the two top reasons to invest in the stock market are the possibility of getting higher returns to your investment and to develop financial discipline.When compared to FD,investing in stocks has resulted in a higher rate of return last 10 yr

7.)What is Technical Analysis (TA) ?

Technical Analysis (TA) is an analysis methodology that uses patterns in market data (primarily price and volume) to identify trends and make predictions.

8.)What is Fundamental Analysis (FA) ?

Fundamental Analysis (FA) is an analysis methodology that finds out the va;luation of that particular stock by examining related economic and financial factors including the balance sheet, strategic initiatives, microeconomic indicators, and consumer behavior.

9.)What Are The Factors That Determine The Price Of The Stock ?

Price of the stock is normally determined by growth prospects and the profitability of the company. Normally, based on the attractiveness of the stock a P/E ratio is assigned by the market. Stock prices also depend on demand and supply as well as news flows.

10.)What Is Equity And Why You Should Invest In It?

Investment in equity is nothing but investing in shares and stocks. You become part owner of the company you are investing in. When you invest in equities, you participate in the growth of the company and hence over longer periods of time, equity creates wealth in the best possible way for investors.
